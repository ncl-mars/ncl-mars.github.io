Resume

Following the cancellation of the biennial of design of Saint-Etienne in 2021, due to the COVID-19, the project of creating a modular exhibition space, accessible online, has been decided by the curators of the exhibition “Le monde, sinon rien”, Benjamin Graindorge and Sophie PÃ¨ne.
