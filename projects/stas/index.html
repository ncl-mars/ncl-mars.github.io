Atlas des bifurcations

Tramway covering using procedural graphics

February 10, 2021

Table of contents

  • Resume
  • Technical aspects

Resume

The contest «un tramway nommé design» organized by the city of Saint-Etienne offered to cover a speed-car of the transportation network (STAS). This happened simultaneously to the design festival of Saint-Etienne.

In collaboration with Thomas Dutoit, graphic designer, we made a system to produce visuals (generative graphics) inspired by the theme of the festival, «bifurcations».

Technical aspects

The program is based on a famous universal Turing machine, the Langton’s ant. By displaying the ants paths with modular shapes, new shapes emerge as the overall image shows how several individuals share a common ground. «The bifurcation’s map», as we named it, won the contest and is now part of the cityscape.
