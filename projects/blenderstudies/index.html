Resume

As I was teaching in a 3D course at the university (ENSCI, les ateliers), I started the development of an add-on for blender, allowing to easily share projects and nodes.

Along this class, students primarly learned modeling, texturing, and rendering. A chapter on terrain design, by drawing height map and painting materials mix was added, as the need came up.
