Resume

In the context of the university degree, I produced an audio-visual installation to immerse the audience into a fictional ecosystem, reacting to visitorâ€™s movements and behaviours. The diploma received the highest honours of the jury in June 2022.

Born from the intention of creating new types of affects with regard to living things, I sought to design a hybrid multisensory space, halfway between a real and virtual environment, acting as a distorting prism of our reality. Questioning the limits of our perceptions, our impact on an ecosystem, or even the use of technology, are all themes that guided me through this proposal.

Technical description

To react to visitors, two Kinects were hidden in the ceiling, face to the ground. A simple blob detection in the depth image of the kinect made an accurate and efficient enough tracking, used for targeting by the particle systems.

Each particule has a voice, distributed among 8 speakers, depending on were they are. A speaker map of the hardware installation is used to spatialize audio

Fiction

It features a fictional living species, the Neume, to which human beings have lost their sensitivity. Due to the ability of these beings to communicate with the rest of living things, they serve as a route of interspecies communication for many Earthlings, and are the guarantors of a natural balance, of a holistic and synergistic functioning. In order to give us the ability to perceive them, I developed special sensors allowing me to represent them in real time in a given space, as well as to transpose their song into a domain audible to humans. We notice that the Neume follow us singing, charged by so many living beings with transmitting messages to us.
